Understanding WAVE Theory

Weaponized Affective Vulnerability Engineering (WAVE) is the coordinated use of emotional pressure, urgency, belonging, secrecy, guilt, fear, and obligation to steer decisions. The attacker’s goal is to control how you feel so they can control what you do.

🧠 Cognitive Overload Exploit limited working memory with panic, guilt, or flattery so you stop verifying and start reacting.
⚡ Behavioral Resonance Shape your emotional state so harmful actions (sending money, sharing access) feel justified and even noble in the moment.
🔄 Synchronized Manipulation Blend belonging, secrecy, and urgency at the same time — pushing you toward isolation, loyalty, and speed so you can't ask for help.
